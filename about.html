Our Story

Where Oxygen Meets Healing Science

OXYNEST is a Mumbai-based clinic founded to address a pressing yet often overlooked public health reality: the cumulative impact of environmental toxins, urban pollution, and chronic stress on cellular oxygen availability and systemic detoxification. By bringing internationally certified therapeutic modalities to India, we have established ourselves as the city’s premier destination for evidence-based, holistic healing.

Our core offerings—Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy, Infrared Sauna, and Hydrocolon Therapy—work synergistically to restore the body’s innate repair mechanisms. Hyperbaric oxygen saturates tissues with the oxygen vital for regeneration; infrared sauna facilitates deep detoxification and circulatory health; hydrocolon therapy supports the safe elimination of accumulated waste. Together, they create the precise physiological environment your body requires to heal.

Our clinical approach integrates advanced medical protocols with a rigorously human-centered philosophy. We recognize that the body possesses a powerful intrinsic ability to repair and regenerate. Our role is to provide world-class tools, expert oversight, and the supportive environment that allow that capacity to function optimally. The result is a therapeutic partnership grounded in science, delivered with unwavering professional integrity.
