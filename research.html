The number of sessions depends on your specific condition and wellness goals. For general wellness and recovery, we typically recommend 10–20 sessions. Yes, HBOT is a very safe therapy when administered by certified professionals in a proper clinical setting. Our facility maintains strict international safety standards, and all sessions are medically supervised. Rare side effects include temporary ear pressure discomfort during compression, which is easily managed. We conduct a thorough medical screening before every patient's first session.

Loose, comfortable cotton clothing or a bathing suit works well for infrared sauna sessions. You'll be provided with a clean towel. Avoid synthetic fabrics which can trap heat uncomfortably. Most clients prefer a minimal coverage bathing suit for maximum skin exposure to infrared rays, which enhances the detoxification effect.
