Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) gives your body something truly extraordinary. You simply lie back and breathe pure oxygen inside a comfortable, pressurized chamber and that gentle pressure allows the healing oxygen to flood every single corner of your body, even areas with poor circulation that normally can't get what they need.

Think of it as waking up your body's deepest natural repair system. This supercharged oxygen calms inflammation, awakens your body's built-in healing cells, and helps you rebuild and recover faster than your body could ever do on its own. It doesn't add anything artificial—it just gives your body the fuel it needs to fix itself, safely and naturally. Your body already knows how to heal. HBOT simply turns up the power.

✅ Do's Before & During HBOT

  • ✓ Stay well hydrated before your session
  • ✓ Wear 100% cotton, loose-fitting clothing
  • ✓ Follow all technician instructions carefully
  • ✓ Inform staff of all medical conditions & medications
  • ✓ Perform ear equalization as instructed
  • ✓ Rest comfortably — meditate, listen to music, or sleep

🚫 Don'ts — For Your Safety

  • ✗ No flammable products (perfumes, hairspray, oils)
  • ✗ No smoking for 24 hours before therapy
  • ✗ Avoid alcohol 24 hours prior to session
  • ✗ No electronic devices inside the chamber
  • ✗ Do not enter with a cold, fever or active infection
  • ✗ No synthetic clothing or metal accessories

Advantages

  • + Non-invasive, drug-free, natural therapy
  • + Addresses root cause through oxygen delivery
  • + Accelerates healing significantly
  • + Medically supervised and safe
  • + Complements other medical treatments

Considerations

  • — Ear or sinus pressure discomfort during pressurization
  • — Not suitable for untreated pneumothorax
  • — Requires multiple sessions for best results
  • — Claustrophobia may be a concern for some
  • — Investment in time (60–90 mins per session)

Infrared Sauna Therapy

Unlike conventional saunas that heat the air, infrared saunas emit radiant heat absorbed directly by the skin and tissues. Far-infrared wavelengths penetrate 4–5 cm beneath the skin surface, gently raising core body temperature and inducing a deep, detoxifying sweat at a more comfortable 45–60°C ambient temperature.

This gentle yet powerful heat activates cellular mitochondria, increases circulation, supports lymphatic drainage and stimulates the release of toxins stored in fat cells — delivering results that regular exercise alone cannot achieve.

✅ Do's for Sauna Therapy

  • ✓ Drink 500ml water before your session
  • ✓ Start with 15 minutes, build to 30–45 over time
  • ✓ Use a clean towel to sit on
  • ✓ Shower with cool water after session
  • ✓ Replenish electrolytes post-session

🚫 Don'ts for Sauna Therapy

  • ✗ Avoid sauna if pregnant or breastfeeding
  • ✗ No alcohol before or after session
  • ✗ Do not exceed recommended session duration
  • ✗ Avoid if you have active skin conditions
  • ✗ Don't eat a heavy meal within 2 hours

Advantages

  • + Lower temperature, more comfortable than steam sauna
  • + Cellular-level detoxification
  • + Backed by robust clinical research
  • + Suitable for most healthy adults

Considerations

  • — Not recommended for certain cardiac conditions
  • — Dehydration risk if proper water intake not maintained
  • — Temporary fatigue after first few sessions

Hydrocolon Therapy

Hydrocolon therapy (colonic hydrotherapy) is a gentle, controlled cleansing of the entire large intestine using warm, purified water. A trained therapist regulates water temperature, pressure and flow through FDA-approved, single-use equipment to thoroughly remove accumulated waste, gas, toxins and mucoid plaque.

Unlike harsh laxatives, colon hydrotherapy works with your body's natural elimination process, supporting the restoration of healthy bowel function, gut microbiome balance and whole-body detoxification.
